## Бочки

- **Red Barrel:** Might
- **Green Barrel:** Endurance
- **Orange Barrel:** Intelligence
- **Yellow Barrel:** Accuracy
- **Blue Barrel:** Personality
- **Purple Barrel:** Speed
- **White Barrel:** Luck

## Cauldrons (Resistances)

* Dirty Cauldron -- +2 Earth Resistance permanent
* Shocking Cauldron -- +2 Air Resistance permanent
* Frosty Cauldron -- +2 Water Resistance permanent
